EXCLUSIVE! Txiki & Fernandinho Call ☎️ | Watch LIFE AFTER FOOTBALL Documentary on City +

Fri 07 Mar 2025, 07:00  •  by Official ManCity
SHARE

City Studios, the club’s unique, world-class creative content and production hub, are back with another compelling documentary, launching on CITY+ at 18:00 (UK) on Thursday.

‘Life After Football’, available on CITY+, explores the challenges, emotions, and life-changing decisions players face when their football careers come to an end.

From club legends to academy graduates, the documentary delves into the personal stories of former Manchester City stars as they navigate the transition from the pitch to new beginnings.

You'll hear from Fernandinho as he wrestles with the decision to retire or push on, guided by Pep Guardiola’s advice. Paul Lake reflects on the heartbreak of a career-ending injury, while Jacob Wright shares how rehabilitation and recovery have transformed in the modern era. David Brightwell discusses his choice to walk away from football to pursue a completely different path as a fireman. Nedum Onuoha opens up about his journey from academy prospect to seasoned professional before finding his voice in football punditry.

The documentary also features Steph Houghton, one of the most respected figures in the women’s game, as she discusses the sacrifices, pressures, and emotions surrounding the later stages of a footballer’s career. As a former England captain and Manchester City icon, she offers a unique perspective on preparing for life after football while still competing at the highest level.

Featuring exclusive interviews with those who have lived through it, expert insights, and never-before-seen footage, ‘Life After Football’ is an emotional and inspiring look at what comes next when the final whistle blows.
